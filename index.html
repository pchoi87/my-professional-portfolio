About Me

Hello! My name is Phil Choi, and I’m a graduate of the Rutgers Full Stack Web Development Coding Bootcamp. I transitioned into web development after a successful career in sales, driven by a long-standing interest in technology that began with creating websites on Angelfire and GeoCities.

At the bootcamp, I gained hands-on experience in full stack development, contributing to projects like "Minigame Masters," where I developed interactive games, and "Pawsitive Vibes," a social media platform for pet owners. I’m committed to continuous learning and am eager to apply my skills in a full-time software engineering role.

I’m motivated by the desire to build a rewarding career, contribute to meaningful projects, and create a stable future where I can express my creativity.
